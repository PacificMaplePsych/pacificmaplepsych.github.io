Psychological Assessments

As leading counsellors and psychologists in Vancouver and across B.C., we offer a wide range of comprehensive psychological testing services. Our assessments cover various common psychiatric issues, including mood and anxiety disorders, PTSD, ADHD, OCD, Bipolar disorder, Schizophrenia, and various phobias.

Additionally, we have a specialization in psychoeducational and learning disorder assessments, with a particular emphasis on assessing intellectual functioning. The results of our assessments play a crucial role in aiding accurate diagnosis and effective treatment planning.

Individual Therapy

Our individual therapy covers the Vancouver and B.C. area and can be conducted physically or virtually. We offer a versatile approach that combines thought-based and emotion-centric explorations. This unique combination creates an atmosphere conducive to self-examination and personal growth.
