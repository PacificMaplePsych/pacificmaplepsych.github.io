Our Services

At Pacific Maple Psych Centre, we understand that every person's journey is unique. That's why we offer a range of personalized services tailored to meet your specific needs, with in-person appointments in downtown Vancouver and virtual care throughout British Columbia.

Whether you are seeking a psychological assessment, individual therapy, group therapy, or Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) assessments, our experienced and compassionate professionals are here to support you every step of the way.

Psychological Assessments

As leading mental health professionals in Vancouver and across BC, we offer a wide range of comprehensive psychological testing services. Our assessments cover various common psychiatric issues, including mood and anxiety disorders, PTSD, ADHD, OCD, Bipolar disorder, Schizophrenia, and various phobias.

Additionally, we have a specialization in psychoeducational and learning disorder assessments, with a particular emphasis on assessing intellectual functioning. You will receive a clear written report and practical recommendations that can support school, workplace, and healthcare planning. The results of our assessments play a crucial role in aiding accurate diagnosis and effective treatment planning.

Individual Therapy

Our individual therapy for adolescents and adults covers the Vancouver and BC area and can be conducted in our downtown office or virtually. We offer a versatile approach that combines thought-based and emotion-centric explorations. This unique combination creates an atmosphere conducive to self-examination and personal growth.

Our team specializes in a wide range of therapeutic modalities. Therapy is tailored to you and may include thought-based treatment such as Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), emotion-focused approaches, exposure-based strategies, and more. We provide a comprehensive approach to treatment that addresses various aspects of any person's experience. Our goal is to help you understand patterns, build practical skills, and create meaningful change in daily life, relationships, and work.
