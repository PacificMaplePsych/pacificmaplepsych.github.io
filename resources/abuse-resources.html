Abuse Resources

If you are looking for abuse resources, domestic violence support, sexual assault support, shelters, crisis lines, legal aid, or victim services, this page is meant to help you get started. It is not meant to be exhaustive. It is meant to make it easier to find practical support when leaving abuse or planning for safety.

General Resources

HelpingSurvivors.org* offers extensive information about domestic violence and sexual harassment, abuse, and assault in a variety of contexts.

DomesticShelters.org offers resources across North America.

Find even more resources at Ending Violence Canada.

Financial abuse is one of the top reasons that it is difficult for people to leave abusers. Read this general guide* and this financial literacy guide* for help.
