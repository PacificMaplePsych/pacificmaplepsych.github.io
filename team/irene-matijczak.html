There are times in life when moving forward feels especially difficult. Relationship stress, work challenges, health concerns, and personal struggles can all build up and make life feel overwhelming or stuck.

I bring over 25 years of experience in the counselling psychology field, and I support children, youth, adults, couples, and families through a wide range of concerns. I work with anxiety, chronic pain, health-related challenges, diabetes and surgery adjustment, self-esteem issues, perfectionism, burnout, workplace bullying, stress, grief, trauma, loss, relationship difficulties, separation and divorce, family conflict, and workplace stress.

My approach is holistic, heart-centered, and forward-thinking. I integrate Counselling Psychology, Executive Coaching, Neuroscience, and Lifestyle Medicine to help support meaningful change. I draw from both traditional counselling methods and innovative approaches so that therapy is practical, collaborative, and focused on real-life progress.

I strive to create a safe, non-judgmental space where I can explore your concerns with you and support you in building insight, resilience, and practical tools. My goal is for you to leave sessions with a renewed sense of hope, clarity, and direction.

I believe that improving quality of life does not always need to take a long time when the right support and tools are in place. I look forward to connecting and supporting you as you move toward a more fulfilling and purposeful life.
