I’m a school psychologist with over a decade of experience supporting children and youth. I specialize in comprehensive psychoeducational assessments, evaluating intellectual, academic, social-emotional, and behavioural functioning. I work closely with families, teachers, and other professionals to address learning, behavioural, and mental health concerns, always using a strengths-based, growth mindset approach. Drawing on my experience within the public school system (K–12), I provide evidence-based recommendations to help students thrive.

Known for my warm, professional, and encouraging nature, I’m proud to be a trusted partner in the educational community. Outside of work, I stay active with yoga, Pilates, strength training, and downhill skiing. I have a passion for live music and always make time for true crime podcasts. I live in Kitsilano with my two cats, who graciously allow my twenty-something son and me to share their home.
